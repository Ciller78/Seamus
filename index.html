About Me

Hi there! I’m Seamus, a charming mixed breed pup with a heart full of energy and a dash of Yorkshire Terrier flair. My dad, Cillian, is from the vibrant city of Dublin, and my mom, Kirsty, brings the lively spirit of Liverpool to my life. With such a fantastic heritage, it’s no wonder I’m full of personality! One of my favorite places in the world is Nefyn, Wales. The beach is my happy place! There’s nothing quite like the feeling of sand between my paws and the salty breeze ruffling my fur as I dash along the shore. I love chasing after seagulls and splashing in the waves—every day is a new adventure! When I’m not enjoying my beach escapades, you’ll find me snuggling up with my humans or showing off my playful side. I’m a little bundle of joy, always ready for a game or a cuddle session. Thanks for visiting my page! I can’t wait to share more of my adventures and spread a little doggy joy!
